About Me

I am a DevOps engineer by trade. I enjoy working in the cloud, but I also have a passion for how websites can be optimized for simplicity and effective access.

As a child I had a knack for taking things apart, but I wasn't always able to put them back together. This curiousity has always been with me, and it applies to my interest in amateur radio and communications technology.


I received my technician-class license from the FCC on June 7th, 2025. Since then I have been actively involved in the amateur radio community, experimenting with different modes of communication and participating in various events.

This site is a way for me to share what I've learned and the projects I am contributing to the amateur radio community.


If you want to check out records of my operations, I invite you to visit my QRZ.com profile.
